Our Mission

Fiercely defending the Northwest we love so dearly.

Founded in 2016, Black Mountain Cycles is a business owned and operated by three close friends who were trying to buy and sell bike parts as a hobby.

Black Mountain Cycles is a community-centric collective where social justice and nature conservancy are paramount. We donate 10% of all profits in a split between various groups, including Seattle Black Lives Matter, Pink Pistols, Nature Consortium, Duwamish Indigenous Rights and many more.

In recent years, the wild Pacific Northwest has become increasingly threatened by clear-cutting and encroachment by real-estate developers. We refuse to believe that it's too late to do something about it.

Our main goal is to give back to the community by organizing events to bring people closer. All of our events raise money and awareness for the preservation of our natural playgrounds, and to help marginalized communities in the Seattle area.

We work with a large number of other businesses, organizations and individuals from Bellingham to Olympia and beyond, and are grateful that so many other entrepreneurs are as passionate about our cause as we are.
